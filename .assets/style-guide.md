## üé® Core Aesthetic

### Terminal/CRT Vibe
- **Color Palette:** Green on black (primary), amber on black (secondary), white on black (accents)
- **Font References:** Monospace, bitmap, terminal-style
- **Effects:** Scan lines, phosphor glow, subtle distortion
- **Layout:** Structured, grid-based, information-dense

### Scientific Documentation
- **Format:** Field reports, specimen dossiers, lab notes
- **Tone:** Clinical, precise, slightly ominous
- **Structure:** Header ‚Üí Classification ‚Üí Content ‚Üí Footer
- **Markings:** Stamps, clearance levels, redaction bars

### Hand-Drawn Elements
- **Style:** Sketch-like, field journal aesthetic
- **Medium:** Ink, pencil, watercolor washes
- **Imperfections:** Welcome and encouraged
- **Subject Matter:** Specimens, maps, diagrams, observations

### Hero Images (README, major pages)
- **Dimensions:** 1200√ó630px (social share), 1920√ó1080px (hero)
- **Format:** PNG (transparent BG), JPG (photographic)
- **Style:** Dark, moody, green/cyan tint, terminal glow

### Specimen Illustrations
- **Dimensions:** 800√ó800px (square), 1200√ó800px (landscape)
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Style:** Scientific illustration + field sketch hybrid
- **Elements:** Annotations, measurements, observation notes

### Maps & Diagrams
- **Dimensions:** 1600√ó1200px, 2400√ó1600px (detailed)
- **Format:** PNG, SVG (for scalability)
- **Style:** Topographic, grid overlay, marked locations
- **Colors:** Muted greens, browns, black borders

### Icons & Badges
- **Dimensions:** 128√ó128px, 256√ó256px
- **Format:** PNG, SVG
- **Style:** Terminal icon aesthetic, pixel art acceptable
- **Palette:** Green (#00FF41), Amber (#FFB000), Black (#0A0A0A)

## üé≠ Mood References

### Visual Inspirations
- **S.T.A.L.K.E.R.** games - Zone documentation, PDA interface
- **Control** - Brutalist bureaucracy, redacted documents
- **Annihilation** - Scientific expedition, biological weirdness
- **Papers, Please** - Terminal interfaces, data entry
- **SCP Foundation** - Clinical documentation, threat levels
- **The Magnus Archives** - Archival horror, catalog system

### Color Psychology
- **Green (#00FF41):** Terminal nostalgia, toxic safety, artificial life
- **Amber (#FFB000):** Warning systems, degraded displays, caution
- **Red (#FF0040):** Danger, classified, infection, death
- **Black (#0A0A0A):** Unknown, void, terminal background
- **White (#F0F0F0):** Data, truth, clinical precision
