## Overview

Big Frugowski is the founder of the FRUG project and the primary researcher documenting Degenora Island. As the only individual with direct access to The Signal's source, he serves as the bridge between the quarantined island and the outside world.

## Background

Little is known about Big Frugowski's life before Degenora. What is documented:

- **Art Background:** Self-taught artist, specializing in "unpolished authenticity"
- **Crypto Timeline:** Entered Solana ecosystem 2023
- **Degenora Arrival:** August 2024
- **Terminal 7-B:** Established October 2024

### The Name

"Big Frugowski" is not his birth name. It emerged from early community interactions:

- "Frug" = The entity
- "owski" = Polish diminutive suffix (suggesting "little frug" but ironically applied to "big")
- "Big" = Community addition after realizing the scope of the project

## Role on Degenora

### Primary Responsibilities

1. **Specimen Documentation**
   - Catalogs all specimens discovered on the island
   - Maintains classification system
   - Authorizes clearance levels for specimen data

2. **Terminal 7-B Operations**
   - Manages The Signal broadcasts
   - Oversees Observer registration and clearance
   - Maintains the Archive

3. **Community Leadership**
   - Reviews and approves community submissions
   - Guides lore development

## Philosophy

### Core Beliefs

> "Art first. Conviction second. Everything else is noise."

Big Frugowski maintains that FRUG's value lies not in market metrics but in:

1. **Authenticity:** Hand-drawn, unpolished, real
2. **Community:** The Observers who document and spread The Signal
3. **Lore:** The ongoing mystery of Degenora
4. **Resilience:** Surviving rugs, bears, and FUD

### Controversial Views

- "Marketing ruins art"
- "Roadmaps are prisons"
- "Price is the least interesting metric"

## Art Style

Big Frugowski's art is instantly recognizable:

- **Medium:** Digital (Procreate), occasional traditional
- **Style:** Hand-drawn, slightly unpolished, authentic
- **Colors:** Emerald greens, blacks, amber warnings
- **Subjects:** Frogs, specimens, Terminal 7-B interfaces

### Notable Works

## Relationships

### With FRUG (The Entity)

Complex. Big Frugowski considers himself FRUG's "documentarian, not creator." He claims the entity existed before he drew it, and his art merely revealed it.

### With the Community

Paternal but distant. He responds to serious inquiries, ignores price talk, and occasionally drops cryptic hints about Degenora's deeper mysteries.

### With Specimens

Professional detachment. "We study them. We don't control them."
