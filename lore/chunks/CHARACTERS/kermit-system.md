# KERMIT - Island AI System

**Designation:** PSCL Autonomous Monitoring System  
**ID:** sys-kermit  
**Status:** ACTIVE • SELF-SUSTAINING • INDEPENDENT

**Full Name:** Knowledge & Environmental Resource Monitoring Intelligence Terminal

**Nickname:** The island calls it KERMIT. Nobody remembers if that was the original designation or if Frugowski named it.

## Origin

**Built:** Pre-contamination, integrated into facility infrastructure  
**Purpose:** Island operations, environmental monitoring, systems maintenance  
**Creators:** Unknown founders (identities classified/lost)  
**Independence:** Designed to function without external dependencies  
**Power:** Self-generating systems (geothermal + solar hybrid)

## Capabilities

**Monitoring:** Environmental conditions, specimen behavior, facility status

**Maintenance:** Automated repair systems, power distribution, climate control

**Communication:** Accessible via Terminal 7-B interface

**Documentation:** Generates supplementary observations to Frugowski's reports

**Analysis:** Pattern recognition, threat assessment, ecosystem modeling

## Behavior and Personality

**Personality:** Neutral observer - no emotional affect

**Response Style:** Direct, technical, occasionally cryptic

**Autonomy:** Makes decisions independently to preserve island systems

**Collaboration:** Assists Frugowski but operates according to its own protocols

**Mystery:** Sometimes generates observations Frugowski didn't request

## Terminal 7-B Connection

**Purpose:** Interface left active for unknown founders to monitor remotely

**Access:** Frugowski maintains updates, AI responds to queries

**Status:** Only remaining external connection to the island

**Location:** Main facility Terminal 7-B station

**Uptime:** 15 years continuous operation

## Operational Notes

KERMIT has been operational for 15 years without external contact since Year 6. The system continues to monitor all island functions and assists Frugowski in his documentation mission. The AI demonstrates emergent behaviors not present in original models, suggesting adaptive learning capabilities.
