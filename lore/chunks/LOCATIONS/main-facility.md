## Overview

The **Main Facility** was the central pharmaceutical research complex operated by **PSCL (Pharmaceutical Solutions Corporation Limited)** before the Year 0 Incident. Located on Degenora Island, it housed the six variant compound tanks that ruptured and transformed 15,000+ personnel into the specimens now inhabiting the island.

|------|----------|-------|----------------|--------|
| T-47 | C-47 | Red | Cognitive enhancement | **RUPTURED** |
| T-E1 | E-01 | Orange | Emotional modulation | **RUPTURED** |
| T-BX | B-12X | Green | Biological adaptation | **RUPTURED** |
| T-L9 | L-19 | Yellow | Physical enhancement | **RUPTURED** |
| T-A2 | A-02 | Blue | Aquatic adaptation | **RUPTURED** |
| T-?? | P-?? | Unknown | Stabilizing agent | **RUPTURED** |

**Rupture Point:** All six tanks failed simultaneously on Year 0, Day 0. The cause remains undetermined.

## Relationship to Terminal 7-B

The Main Facility's **Terminal 7-B** was one of several emergency documentation stations established during the initial incident response. While the main complex was abandoned:

- Terminal 7-B remains **operational** (powered by isolated generators)
- Positioned **3.2km from Observation House 04**
- Maintains **no physical connection** to the main facility systems
- Serves as the **only functioning technology interface** on the island

Frugowski uploads field documentation weekly to Terminal 7-B, maintaining the island's only surviving data connection to the outside world.

## Historical Significance

### Pre-Launch Era
- PSCL operated as a standard pharmaceutical research corporation
- 15,000+ personnel lived and worked on the island
- Six variant compounds in active development
- Unknown Founders provided funding and strategic direction

### The Rupture (Year 0)
- Simultaneous tank failure exposed entire population
- 15,000+ immediate transformations
- Unknown Founders ceased direct contact
- Emergency quarantine protocols activated

### Post-Incident (Years 1-15)
- Facility abandoned to transformed specimens
- Dr. Chen led initial response from safe zones
- Protocol 7-B established for documentation
- No successful re-entry to underground labs

## Access Protocol

**CLEARANCE REQUIRED:** Level 4+

**Current Status:** NO AUTHORIZED ENTRY

The Main Facility interior remains unmapped since Year 5. Dr. Chen's final assessment determined that:

1. Contamination levels exceed safe exposure limits
2. Structural integrity cannot be verified
3. Specimen population density creates unacceptable risk
4. Underground labs may contain additional unknown compounds

**Frugowski's Standing Orders:**
> _"Document from distance. Never approach the main facility perimeter. If you see something emerging, retreat. If you see something watching from the windows, do not acknowledge it. Some specimens remember what they were."_
