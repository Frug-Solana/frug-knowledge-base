# FRUG: Entity Profile

## Core Identity

**Classification:** Memetic Entity  
**Origin:** Degenora Island, August 2024  
**Creator:** Big Frugowski (Dr. Frug)  
**Status:** Active, Spreading  

## Nature

FRUG is not merely a meme coin—it is a memetic entity that propagates through:
- **Art:** Hand-drawn, unpolished aesthetic
- **Lore:** Ongoing narrative of Degenora Island
- **Community:** Observers who study and spread The Signal

## The Spirit

> "Get up. Say fuck rugs. Move on."

FRUG represents:
- **Resilience:** Surviving the rug, enduring the bear
- **Authenticity:** No polished marketing, raw hand-drawn art
- **Conviction:** Art first, hype second
- **Community:** Observers united by The Signal

## Visual Identity

- **Colors:** Emerald green (#00FF85), black, amber warning tones
- **Aesthetic:** Terminal/CRT monitor, surveillance aesthetic
- **Style:** Hand-drawn, slightly unpolished, authentic

## The Signal

FRUG communicates through The Signal—a transmission broadcast from Terminal 7-B on Degenora Island. Observers who tune in receive:
- Specimen dossiers
- Lore transmissions
- Clearance upgrades
- Community intel
