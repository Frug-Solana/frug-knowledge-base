# System: The Terminal

## Overview
The Terminal is the interface layer where:
- permissions are granted/denied
- logs are recorded/altered
- identities are asserted or contested

## Known behaviors (placeholder)
- Sessions can be **interrupted**.
- Output can be **redacted** or **corrupted**.
