# Glossary

Quick reference for FRUG/Degenora terminology.

## A

**Absentee** — A departed player whose shadow continues to observe through the Absentee Protocol.

**Absentee Protocol** — System allowing departed players to passively contribute and earn rewards.

## B

**Big Frug** — Legendary first observer, founder of Terminal 7-B.

## C

**Canon** — Verified, official truth in the Degenora Archive.

**Chunk** — A discrete lore unit with stable ID per the [schema](../lore/schema.md).

**Clearance** — Access level (1-5) determining available content and features.

**Corrupted Log** — Partially damaged or altered documentation. Handle with caution.

## D

**Degenora** — The island ecosystem, setting of the FRUG universe.

## E

**Echo** — Signal phenomenon mimicking voices or events. Not the original—something else wearing a familiar shape.

## F

**Field Report** — First-hand observations from Observers.

**FRUG** — The distributed consciousness connecting all Observers.

**Frugowski** — Archivist who maintained Terminal 7-B. Status: Unknown.

## G

**Grafting** — Process of specimens incorporating Signal energy.

## K

**Kermit** — AI system managing Terminal 7-B communications. Prone to sarcasm.

## L

**Lore Unlock** — System for time-based, clearance-gated, or coordinate-based content discovery.

## M

**Mutation** — Biological changes from Signal exposure.

## O

**Observer** — A participant who documents and studies Degenora.

**Observation House** — Outpost where Observers rest and access Terminal systems.

## P

**PILL** — Mysterious substance found on Degenora. Primary in-game currency.

## S

**Signal, The** — The mysterious force permeating Degenora. Origin unknown.

**Specimen** — Any documented creature, plant, or entity on Degenora.

**Swamp, The** — Central ecosystem of Degenora. Most Signal activity occurs here.

## T

**Terminal 7-B** — Central communications hub for Degenora. Administered by Kermit.

**Toaddy** — AI assistant for onboarding and help. Less cryptic than Kermit.

**Truth Level** — Metadata indicating reliability: `canon`, `record`, `rumor`, `corrupted`, `redacted`.

## Y

**Year 0** — The year of the Incident that brought the Signal to Degenora.

**Year 15** — Current year. The Signal has been broadcasting for 15 years.
